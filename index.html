> Checklist before starting:

  1. Ensure you're in a quiet, well-lit place where you won't be disturbed.
  2. Use a computer or laptop with a stable internet connection.
  3. Your screen must be at least 20 inches with a resolution of 1920x1080 or higher. Ensure your speakers are on.
  4. Only one webcam should be connected. If you're using a laptop, unplug any external webcams. You must enable webcam access to qualify for payment.
  5. Make sure your browser window is maximized when answering the questionnaire.

> Troubleshooting:

  • If you are stuck after the calibration (the ‘Next’ button does not appear), try zooming out so the interface becomes smaller in your browser. To zoom out on Windows, press Ctrl and - (minus) on your keyboard. Repeat until the interface fits properly, and the ‘Next’ button is visible.

> What is the task?

Over THREE days, you’ll watch daily videos of automated vehicles during 8-minute morning and evening drives.

The task requires your participation twice a day:

  • Once in the morning, between 7 am and 12 pm
  • Once in the evening, between 4 pm and 9 pm

These times follow your local timezone. You’ll answer a questionnaire each time, totaling six across the study.

Each questionnaire takes about 13 minutes to complete. After finishing, you’ll get a link to return to this page, which will track your progress.
